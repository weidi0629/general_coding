6.2 Maintaining the heap property

MAX-HEAPIFY.A; i /
1 l D LEFT.i /
2 r D RIGHT.i /
3   if l <=  A:heap-size and A[l] > A[i] //两个儿子里面取个最大的
4   largest =  l
5 else largest = i
6 if r <= A:heap-size and A[r] > = A[largest] 
7   largest  = r
8 if largest ¤ i
9   exchange A[i] with with A[largest]
10  MAX-HEAPIFY(A; largest)
